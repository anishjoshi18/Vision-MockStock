INTRODUCTION AND OBJECTIVE:

 ‘You are neither right nor wrong because other people agree with you. You are right because your facts are right and your reasoning is right and that’s the only thing that makes you right.’ Accordingly invest in yourself, your career is the engine of your wealth. As we know the United States have developed a huge platform to invest capital and get more profit by using intelligence, it’s called stock market and this trend of stock marketing is increasing in India as well day by day. Looking towards general scenario of market, the knowledge of share market is important. So, test yourself for endurance and survival, because you grow only if you know.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:

 o There would be total two rounds out of which, first would be consisting of group discussion and surprise round depending upon share market.
o Short listing would be done from first round and the shortlisted candidates would be applicable for second round.
o Second Round will be virtual stock exchange simulation round. Players are expected to perform like real time investors and maximize their gains over many derivatives.
o All teams and their respective members should reach the event place in time. Tardiness may be considered as grounds for disqualification.
o All teams must maintain decorum at all times. Organizers can disqualify any team that behaves inappropriately.
o In case of disputes, the decision of event head and judges will be final and binding to all.
o The organizers reserve all rights to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit. The change in rules, if any, will be highlighted on the website / forum.
